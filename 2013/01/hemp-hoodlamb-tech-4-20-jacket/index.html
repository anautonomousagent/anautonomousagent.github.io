Hemp Hoodlamb Tech 4-20 Jacket

By anautonomousagent

On January 3, 2013

In clothing, earth, hemp, jacket, website

Hemp Hoodlamb Tech 4-20 Jacket.  Such a great jacket. Very warm and comfortable. About ten pockets that I can find, including two with magnetic buttons. Made from hemp and recycled plastic, it has a strong and durable feel. Highly recommended as a quality jacket, worth every penny, that will last many years. Might be cheaper here.
