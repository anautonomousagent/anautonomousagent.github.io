TrueCrypt

By anautonomousagent

On January 10, 2013

In anonymity, cyber, download, truecrypt, website

TrueCrypt will protect a file, hard drive, or operating system. This simple, free, and open-source software will provide a high level of security to prevent unauthorized access. Highly recommended; at least until quantum computing becomes a reality. TrueCrypt is no longer secure as of 2014. A good replacement for TrueCrypt is VeraCrypt. It even opens old TrueCrypt volumes.
