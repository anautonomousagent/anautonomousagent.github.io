For example, the ones that I bought, the Sennheiser HD 598s are known to be good (considering their price) for listening to the higher side of the spectrum; i.e., vocals and other high frequency sounds. I must say that I love my Sennheiser HD 598s. Sennheiser produces a couple of other highly rated headphones, including the HD 650 and HD 800. Here are some other headphones which have good reviews:  Beyerdynamic DT 770, AKG K702, Grado SR60, Grado RS1, and the ATH M50.
